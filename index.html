GCI - Global Crisis Interventions

GCI (Global Crisis Interventions) is a 501(c)(3) medical and disaster relief organization supporting charitable activities in nations affected by disasters.

Learn More

Our Mission

We provide medical aid, disaster relief, and support to communities impacted by crises, working to improve lives and restore hope across affected regions.

Learn More

AREAS OF INTERVENTIONS

GLOBAL HEALTH

We rebuild, renovate and rehabilitate medical centers (Medicines, supplies and equipment) and schools in nations affected by disaster. We work with our partners and local communities during all phases of the disaster and any outbreak to increase access to healthcare, improve malnutrition and protect people against diseases. We train local medical staff to provide urgent care and Psychological support to the victims of disaster. GCI team with communities to provide clearer, cleaner and safer water, appropriate sanitation and improve their hygiene. To target contaminants from water, we use certain water treatment methods to remove physical and chemical impurities.

ENVIRONMENT AND SUSTAINABLE DEVELOPMENT

ENVIRONMENTAL PROTECTION - SUSTAINABILITY- CLIMATE CHANGE Many nations are subjects to increasing temperature and inconsistent rain. The socio-economic threats are even higher. The food security is affected due to crop losses. Food insecurity, water scarcity, natural disasters and inhospitable temperature are the effects of climate change that place people at risk in many African nations. We at GCI, we protect the ecosystem and reforestation of endangers species develop crop diversity (Dietary variety) implement reforestation projects. We promote waste prevention and recycling nation wide.

DISASTER RELIEF / HUMANITARIAN ASSISTANCE

We provide humanitarian assistance (food, medications, med supplies, and equipment) to save lives, alleviate human suffering, and reduce the socio-economic impacts of natural, man-made disasters and armed conflicts in affected nations worldwide. We provide relief to people forced to flee their homeland due to war, political unrest, famine, natural or man-made disaster. At GCI, we provide assistance before, during and after a crisis; from readiness and response, to relief and recovery. Our emergency preparedness and disaster management cycle is divided in three divisions: Preparedness and prevention division, Response division, Recovery and Mitigation division.

PEACE BUILDING

In war zone, we support local people/ use community-based approach to Stop violence escalating and build long term peace. We use the 3 Rs approach: Rehabilitation, Reintegration and Reinsertion of Ex- combatants within their community. We fight against Gender based violence (GBV). We provide free urgent care and Psychotherapy to all women, men and children affected by the conflict. We organize conferences on conflict resolution and Peace Building once a year.

SUSTAINABLE AGRICULTURE & CONSERVATION

The Everlasting Solution for Nutrition / Food Security. Many children in impoverished countries suffer from malnutrition due to lack of food and many medications need to be taken with food. From cash assistance received from people of good will like you, we buy food locally and feed the malnourished children. Farmers are the solution to hunger and poverty. At GCI, we train local people in efficient agriculture protection. We provide startup capital and financing in order to fight rural poverty and malnutrition in the aftermath. We promote and support global food security. To curb the issue of food shortage and malnutrition in nations affected by disaster, we establish the SVI (System of Vegetables Intensification). We buy land and grow vegetables (eggplants, onions, carrot, cabbage, peas, beans, tomatoes, corn, potatoes, and so on).
